About Us

JKUSA stands for the "Jomo Kenyatta University Students Association." It is typically a student-led organization representing the interests and welfare of students at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT) in Kenya. JKUSA serves as the official student government or union, aiming to create a positive and supportive environment for students during their university journey.

JKUSA Events.

JKUSA events foster unity, offer networking and inspire growth.

Our mission is clear: to create a supportive and inclusive environment where students can excel academically, grow personally, and make lifelong connections. We believe in your potential, and we're committed to helping you achieve greatness.

What We Offer

  • Student Advocacy: We stand up for your rights, ensuring your concerns are addressed at every level.
  • Networking: Connect with fellow students, alumni, and industry leaders.
  • Personal Growth: Access resources and opportunities for personal and professional development.
  • Events: Join us in exciting events, workshops, and community projects.
  • Friendship and Fun: Forge lasting friendships and create memorable experiences outside the classroom.
